# Company Profile

Company Name: NovaTech Components LLC

Industry: Small manufacturing / wholesale distribution

Location: Lynchburg, Virginia

Close Month: September 2026

Accounting Method: Accrual Basis

Inventory Method: FIFO

Depreciation Method: Straight-Line

Employees: 18

Estimated Annual Revenue: $4.8 million

Business Description:
NovaTech Components LLC buys, assembles, and sells computer accessory components to electronics retailers, computer repair shops, and small business technology vendors. The company sells USB-C hubs, docking stations, charging cables, wireless keyboard and mouse bundles, monitor adapters, and small repair kits.

Project Scenario:
For this project, I acted as the staff accountant responsible for completing the September 2026 month-end close. The close process included recording transactions, posting journal entries, preparing the general ledger, reconciling cash activity, recording adjusting entries, preparing trial balances, and producing financial statements.

Project Objective:
The objective of this project is to demonstrate practical accounting skills used in staff accountant, audit associate, and financial analyst roles. The project shows the full accounting cycle from raw transaction activity to final financial reporting.
